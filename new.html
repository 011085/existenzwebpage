new album - data -

- d a t a - is an album recorded in 2024 that mixes random compositions generated by means of a micro-modular synthesizer and a feedback configuration made by a mixer and effects pedals.



These are compositions influenced by experimentation and the bleep sound of electronic music used by artists such as Peter Zinovieff, Delia Derbyshire, Kraftwerk, Sweet Exorcist and LFO , among others.

The concept of the mini album takes the theme of data, how it is stored, in which places, its use and representation, and imagines a fiction through sound.

Equipment Used: Korg Volca micro-modular/Behringer Xenix 1204 usb /MXR '78 distortion/DOD digital delay DFX9/ Korg peq-1/Stagg looper/Daw Ardour/LSP plugins
