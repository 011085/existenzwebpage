Existenz is an experimental audiovisual project that began in 2.017. A solo effort by Yamil Noise, it has participated in festivals and exhibitions, such as "Una Noche En Los Museos", exhibit by Arte Sonoro "Universidad de Quilmes" that took place in Museo de Artes Visuales V. Roverano in Quilmes. Other examples are works shown in Histórico Museo Comunitario de Artes Visuales of Florencio Varela, in La Cúpula Media Lab, Córdoba, and Intermediale Festival of Audiovisuale forms, exhibition "Videosynthezy", Poland.

So far, Existenz has released 3 albums, IDRA (2018), NADA (2020) & d a t a (2025), and took part in different netlabels like Capitan Mou, BorderTapes, Isla Visión, Feroz Device and Veinte33 Records. Also collaborated with artists, namely Aux, Zigo Rayopineal and Mi Propia Necrológica. The preferred method of making sounds is by using feedback, combined with effects and field recordings, to generate ambiences that include low frequencies, static, noise and overall randomness. The project's title is a direct reference to David Cronenberg's film "eXistenZ" (1999), about a virtual reality game.

By Thanassis.
